I will make London too hot to hold you;—and if I do not find you I will go with my story to every friend you have. I have now told you as exactly as I can the condition of my mind. Winifred Hurtle.

Having written this she again read the short note, and again gave way to violent tears. But on that day she sent no letter. On the following morning she wrote a third, and sent that. This was the third letter:—

Yes. Come.

W. H.
